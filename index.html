关于About

多年从事随钻泥浆高速信息遥传研究，参与国家重点研发计划。 研究方向涵盖自适应信号处理、噪声对消与实时嵌入式系统。擅长用 MATLAB/Python 建立多源噪声模型，在极端通信环境中还原信号的本来面貌。 Years of research on high-speed mud pulse telemetry, National Key R&D Program. Adaptive signal processing, noise cancellation, and real-time embedded systems. Building multi-source noise models in MATLAB/Python to recover signals in extreme communication environments.
