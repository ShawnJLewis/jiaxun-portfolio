About关于

I'm a PhD candidate at Zhejiang University's Ocean Sensing & Networking Institute. My work sits at the intersection of adaptive signal processing, noise cancellation, and real-time embedded systems — applied to extreme communication environments from kilometers-deep drilling mud to coastal underwater channels.

浙江大学海洋传感与网络研究所博士研究生。研究方向为自适应信号处理、噪声对消与实时嵌入式系统——从数千米深的钻井泥浆到近海水声信道，致力于在极端通信环境中还原信号的本来面貌。

Currently exploring audio algorithm development. I've worked across MATLAB, Python, Verilog/FPGA, and embedded Linux/STM32 stacks, and I care about algorithms that survive contact with the real world.

目前研究方向延伸至音频算法开发。技术栈覆盖 MATLAB、Python、Verilog/FPGA 与嵌入式 Linux/STM32，信奉"能落地才有价值"。
